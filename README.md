
# E-commerce Customer Segmentation & Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview & Goal
The primary objective of this project is to maximise retail business revenue by identifying high-value customer segments and analysing overall sales performance. By leveraging **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis**, this project categorizes customers into actionable segments like **"Champions," "Big Spenders," and "At Risk"** to help the marketing team design targeted retention and acquisition strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Process
I built an end-to-end data pipeline to transform raw, unstructured transactional data into interactive business insights:

1. **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing (Python):** * Handled missing values and removed duplicate records using **Pandas** and **NumPy**.
   * Standardized date-time columns and filtered out negative/cancelled transactions to ensure data integrity.
2. **Data Manipulation & Metrics (SQL):** * Wrote optimized SQL queries to aggregate sales, calculate purchase frequency, and compute individual customer monetary value.
3. **Data Visualization & Analytics (Power BI):** * Designed an interactive executive dashboard mapping out global revenue trends.
   * Implemented DAX measures to calculate RFM scores and built user-friendly segment breakdowns.

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights
* **Peak Sales Season:** **November** experienced the highest sales volume and revenue spike throughout the analyzed period, signaling a massive holiday-season demand.
* **Top Revenue Generator:** The **"Regency Cake Stand 3 Tier"** emerged as the highest-selling product, contributing significantly to the overall top-line growth.
* **Customer Behavior:** **"New & Promising"** customers are driving a massive chunk of recent revenue, indicating great initial acquisition but highlighting a critical need for loyalty programs to prevent them from slipping into the **"At Risk"** pool.

---

## 💡 Key Takeaways & Recommendations
* **Retention Strategy:** Target the **"At Risk"** customer group with personalized email marketing campaigns and exclusive discounts before they completely churn.
* **Inventory Management:** Stock up heavily on top-tier products like the *Regency Cake Stand* well in advance of the Q4 holiday rush (specifically October-November).
